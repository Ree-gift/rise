Coping Strategies

Reframe the Rejection

View each rejection as redirection to something better suited for you. Companies reject candidates for countless reasons that have nothing to do with your worth.

Try this:

Write down 3 things this experience taught you.

The 24-Hour Rule

Give yourself 24 hours to feel disappointed, then channel that energy into your next application. Acknowledge your emotions, but dont let them define your timeline.

Try this:

Set a timer for 24 hours, then update your resume or apply to a new role.

Celebrate the Effort

Applying is an achievement in itself. Each application represents courage and effort. Track your applications and celebrate milestones.

Try this:

Reward yourself for every 10 applications submitted.

Build Your Support Network

Connect with others who understand the job search struggle. Share your experiences, vent safely, and learn from others journeys.

Try this:

Share your story in the community or reach out to a mentor.
